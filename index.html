The Hotel

Italy's Alpine secret — designed for those who know what they want

Nestled in Algund above Merano, at the gateway to the South Tyrol Dolomites, Hotel Maria Theresia is a place where quality wins over compromise. An adults-only 4-star hotel where the Italian Alps meet Mediterranean ease — just a 2-hour drive from Verona or Innsbruck airport, yet a world away from ordinary.

Surrounded by terraced vineyards, apple orchards and rose gardens, with the Dolomite peaks as your backdrop — this is northern Italy at its most authentic. Still largely undiscovered by Dutch, Belgian and Danish travellers, Algund sits at just 350 m altitude with a uniquely warm microclimate: mild, sunny and sheltered by the Alps.

The city of Merano — with its historic promenade, thermal baths, designer boutiques and large shopping centres — is just 10 minutes away by bus from the hotel. Everything you need, exactly when you want it.

The Destination

Algund, South Tyrol — where the Dolomites begin

At 350 m altitude, Algund sits in one of Europe's most privileged locations: sheltered by the Alps, warmed by the Italian sun, and surrounded by some of the most dramatic mountain scenery on the continent.

Vineyards & Apple Orchards
Walk through the Sonnenberg vineyard trail and taste Alto Adige wines. Apple orchards stretch across the valley below — the scent alone is worth the trip.
Merano — 10 Min Away
Historic promenade, Terme Merano thermal baths, boutiques, and large shopping centres — all just 10 minutes by bus from the hotel door.
Dolomites & Alpine Hikes
The UNESCO-listed Dolomites are on your doorstep. From gentle valley walks to dramatic ridge hikes — every level, every landscape.
Waalwege Irrigation Trails
These ancient water channels create magical flat walking paths through the landscape — shaded, peaceful, and uniquely South Tyrolean.

Getting Here

Closer than you think — from the Netherlands, Belgium & Denmark

Whether you drive, fly or take the train — Algund is surprisingly accessible from northern Europe.

By Car
From Amsterdam or Brussels, roughly 1,000 km — mostly motorway. A22 Brenner motorway through Austria, exit Bolzano-Sud, then MEBO expressway to the Lagundo exit. 5 minutes to the hotel. Free garage parking included. EV charging on-site.
By Plane
Seasonal direct flights from Amsterdam (AMS) to Verona or Innsbruck — then a scenic 1.5–2 hr drive through the Alps. Brussels (BRU) and Copenhagen (CPH) connect well via Munich, Innsbruck or Verona.
By Train
Travel via Munich or Innsbruck to Merano station. Local bus 213 or 235 reaches Algund in 10 minutes — the stop is just 50 m from the hotel. No car needed for the entire journey.
