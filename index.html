The Destination

Why Algund? Because Merano was just the beginning.

Sitting 350 m above Merano, Algund is one of South Tyrol's best-kept secrets — a sun-blessed village where Mediterranean palms meet Alpine peaks, and where the pace of life is exactly what you need it to be.

🍷
Wine & Orchards
Walk through the Sonnenberg vineyard trail and taste some of Italy's finest Alto Adige wines. Apple orchards stretch as far as the eye can see.
🏛️
Merano City & Thermal Baths
Just minutes from the hotel: the stunning Terme Merano thermal baths, the historic promenade, and excellent restaurants and shopping.
🚶
Waalwege Irrigation Trails
These ancient irrigation channels create magical walking paths through the landscape — flat, shaded, and utterly tranquil. A South Tyrolean icon.
🎫
AlgundCard Included
Free public transport across South Tyrol, free cable cars, and discounts on pools, gardens, and attractions — all included in your stay.

Getting Here

Closer than you think — from the Netherlands, Belgium & Denmark

Whether you prefer the freedom of the road, the ease of a flight, or the romance of the train, Algund is surprisingly accessible from northern Europe.

🚗
By Car
From Amsterdam, Brussels, or Copenhagen, it's a scenic drive of roughly 1,000 – 1,400 km — mostly highway. Take the A22 Brenner motorway through Austria, exit at Bolzano-Sud, then the MEBO expressway to the Lagundo exit. Only 5 minutes to the hotel. Free parking included.
✈️
By Plane
Seasonal direct flights from Amsterdam (AMS) to Verona or Innsbruck. From either airport, it's a beautiful 1.5–2 hour drive through the Alps to Algund. Brussels (BRU) and Copenhagen (CPH) also connect well via Verona, Munich, or Innsbruck.
🚂
By Train
Travel via major hubs like Munich or Innsbruck to Merano station. From there, local buses (213 or 235) reach Algund in about 10 minutes — no car required for the whole journey. A scenic and stress-free alternative.
