Hotel Algund – near Merano, South Tyrol

Where the Alps meet
Italian warmth

Nestled in the rose-scented village of Algund, just ten minutes from Merano, Hotel Maria Theresia is a thoughtfully curated adults-only retreat at 350 metres altitude. The exceptional microclimate — shielded by the Alps, kissed by Mediterranean warmth — makes this one of South Tyrol’s most coveted corners.

Here, mornings begin with a lavish breakfast overlooking vineyards and apple orchards. Evenings unfold with five gourmet courses. And the days? Left entirely to you — whether you’re hiking toward the Dolomites, cycling the ancient Waalwege water channels, or simply floating in our heated panorama pool at 30°C.

Ten exclusive rooms. No children. Just the quiet pleasure of a place designed for those who know exactly what they want.

The Region

Everything within reach —
nothing to organise

The AlgundCard comes free with every stay: unlimited trains, buses, and cable cars across all of South Tyrol. Your host Wolfgang leads Tuesday excursions — just show up.

Hiking & Dolomites
Trails for every level begin minutes from the hotel. Wolfgang's guided Tuesday hikes uncover the landscape's best-kept secrets — including viewpoints you won't find on any map.
Merano in 10 Minutes
The elegant spa town of Merano is just two stops away by bus. Terme Merano thermal baths, the famous promenade, boutique shopping, and some of South Tyrol’s finest restaurants.
Wine, Orchards & Törggele
Algund sits in the heart of apple and vine country. In autumn, the Törggele tradition — new wine, roasted chestnuts, and farmhouse feasts — is unlike anything else in the Alps.
E-Biking & Waalwege
Borrow an e-bike and follow the ancient Waalwege irrigation channels through the terraced orchards above Merano. The views are extraordinary, and the effort is entirely optional.
